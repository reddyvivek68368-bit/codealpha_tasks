📉 **Project Completed: Unemployment Analysis in India | CodeAlpha Data Science Internship — Task 2**

Just wrapped up my second project from the CodeAlpha Data Science
Internship — analyzing how COVID-19's lockdown affected unemployment
across Indian states, and building a model to predict unemployment rate
from key economic indicators.

📊 **Headline finding:** national average unemployment rate jumped from
**9.48% to 14.92%** after the March 2020 lockdown — a **57.3% relative
increase** — peaking above 23% in June 2020.

🔧 **What I built:**
✅ Cleaned and merged two real-world CMIE datasets (handling missing
   rows, whitespace inconsistencies, and COVID-era outliers carefully)
✅ In-depth EDA: national trends, state comparisons, urban vs rural,
   zone-wise analysis, and a state × month heatmap
✅ Trained & compared 5 regression models (Linear, Ridge, Decision Tree,
   Random Forest, Gradient Boosting)
✅ Selected Random Forest as the best model (R² = 0.70)
✅ Built an interactive **Streamlit dashboard** with filters, a dedicated
   COVID-impact tab, and a live prediction tool

🛠️ **Tech stack:** Python, pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib, seaborn,
Plotly, Streamlit
